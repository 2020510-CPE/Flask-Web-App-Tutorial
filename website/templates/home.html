A L E R T

ALERT is equipped with state of the art traffic counting capabilities, which facilitate data driven analysis of traffic patterns to improve road management and safety measures. ALERT is a robust solution that aims to improve efficiency and safety on the road network through combining real time recognition of an emergency vehicle with accurate traffic monitoring.
